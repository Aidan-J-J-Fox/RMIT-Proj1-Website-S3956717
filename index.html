Interest in IT

My interest in IT began at an early age. My grandmother had a PC (Personal Computers) and games for the kids to play, but as I found out later, those were her PC games. Titles like Diablo and Dungeon Keeper were my first games. Once my parents got their own computer it was over, I had grown far too interested in that cream shelled box.

once I got to high school, I expressed an interest in buying a better computer to run newer games, and a friend of mine (who was incredibly talented with IT) pointed out I could save money by building it myself, which at the time felt taboo, I made sure to learn everything I could about building computers and solving random PC related issues that I could.

I maintain my knowledge of computer hardware so that it is as current as a consumer can get which means a lot of Linus tech tips videos and articles that pop up in my media aggregate.
